Life as American college students

During our time in the US, we emersed ourselves in many aspects of college life...

Greek Life

Sorority and fraternity life was a big part of making our year so enjoyable! Despite the stereotypes that the movies portray, being part of a sorority is a unique experience and gave us the opportunity to make a wide circle of friends. There are many different sororities on campus, all of which carry different values, and cater to different personalities, however they all work hard to encourage their members to succeed socially and academically.

Sports

In the US, the support for college sports teams is incredible! Basketball and American Football are the most popular sports and there is an amazing atmosphere and sense of pride on campus following a big win. Rivalry can get intense, and many of the games are streamed live on TV to rally national support, but experiencing it first hand in the sports arenas is a must!

Academics

One of the hardest obstacles we had to overcome was to adjust to the American academic system. The methods of teaching and assessment are very different to those at a British university, with 17 week terms and weekly exams.
